The Problem

Most totes are either beautiful and impractical, or practical and forgettable. You end up juggling two bags — one for your laptop, one you actually like. The Everyday Tote is built to end that compromise. It carries your 15" laptop, your lunch, your gym kit, your book, your chargers, and your dignity — all at once.

Water Resistant
